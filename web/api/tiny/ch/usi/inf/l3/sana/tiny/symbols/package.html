  1. trait Symbol extends AnyRef

    The supertype of all symbols.

    The supertype of all symbols. A symbol is attached to an AST and stores its information. Symbols are used for name resolution.

  2. trait TermSymbol extends Symbol

    The supertype of all term-symbol.

    The supertype of all term-symbol. Term-symbols are attached to the trees that do not introduce a new type, but a new term like fields, packages and methods.

  3. trait TypeSymbol extends Symbol

    The supertype of all type-symbol.

    The supertype of all type-symbol. Type-symbols are attached to the trees that introduce a new type, like classes and interfaces.
