trait SymbolAssignerComponent extends TransformationComponent[Tree, Tree]

Assigns symbols to definition trees and the trees that introduce a new scope, and assigns contextual owners to all trees. For understanding contextual owner please refer to tiny.symbols.Symbol.owner.

Parent trees communicate with their child trees (i.e. they tell them who their parents is) using their 

owner

field. This field is added to trees via tiny.ast.augmenters.AugmentedTree.owner and tiny.ast.augmenters.AugmentedTree.owner_= methods.
